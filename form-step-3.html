Did you pay any W-2 wages in 2020 or 2021?

The ERC credit is calculated as a percentage of W-2 wages paid - 1099 wages do not qualify. Note: The wages of majority owners (over 50%) and their immediate relatives (even if paid W-2) do not qualify for the ERC program. Wages for all other W-2 employees still qualify.

List majority owners and relatives

List the first and last names of all majority owners (over 50%) and their family members contained in the payroll reports. Please keep in mind that constructive ownership rule applies. (Example: Two brothers own a business 50/50, because they are related, the constructive ownership rule applies and they are both considered majority owners.) Enter none if this does not apply.
